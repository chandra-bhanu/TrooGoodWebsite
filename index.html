ABOUT OUR

CHAMP

With an ambition of feeding a billion lives,
TrooGood was founded in 2018.
Since it's about norusihing and enriching lives, we had to do good. Thus, we chose Millets- the superfood.

Millets, in contrast to rice and wheat, are not only a good source of energy and major nutrients, including protein, but are also a good source of micronutrients such as vitamins, including vitamins A, B, D, E, niacin, pyridoxine, antioxidants, iron and zinc.

Our road to becoming India’s Biggest Millet Snack Company began from the roots. Cultivating, producing and choosing the finest millet grown locally by farmers we created the TrooGood Millet Chikki Bar.

Snacking
Reimagined

Does snacking always have to be unhealthy?
A Big no.

We reimagined snacking with the power of Millet. Our Troo Good Millet Chikki Bars are snacks that are packed with wholesome goodness in equal and right proportions.

The nutritional blend of Ragi, Jowar and Pearl millet with Peanuts became of apt snacking choice.
